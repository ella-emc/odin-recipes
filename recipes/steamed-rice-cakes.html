Steamed Rice Cakes

Description

Steamed rice cake in the Philippines, also known as puto, is a type of snack that is made of a special type of rice. Kanin in Filipino means 'rice,' hence steamed rice cake is also known in the country as kakanin. Alongside rice, steamed rice cake is also produced with sugar, milk, and flour. Steamed rice cakes are fluffy and can come in different colors. The strip of cheese on top only adds to its rich taste. Read on for more details on how to prepare this special delicacy.
